Our Story

Kendia began with a simple vision: to bring the authentic taste and nutrition of naturally raised poultry and fresh eggs to our community. As a family-owned business, we care deeply about every aspect of our farm.

We combine traditional farming wisdom with modern hygiene practices. Our commitment to freshness, indigenous breeds, and proper hygienic packaging is what sets Kendia apart.
